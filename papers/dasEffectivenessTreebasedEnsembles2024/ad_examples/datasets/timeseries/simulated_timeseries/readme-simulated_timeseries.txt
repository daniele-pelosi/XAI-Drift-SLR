The timeseries here was generated synthetically by timeseries/simulate_timeseries.py.

There are three 'activities'.

First column in all files is the index column (zero-indexed).
Second column of activities_2000.csv is the activity number (0|1|2)
Second column of samples_2000.csv is the timeseries value
Second column of starts_2000.csv is the time at which an activity starts
